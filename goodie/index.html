AvailabilIT NFC keychain

This dandelion NFC keychain symbolises our idea of AvailabilIT. A single seed can drift, land, and grow into something bigger, just like a clever idea shared with the right people. Consider this your pocket-sized reminder that big things can start small.

What can you use it for?

In principle, you can use it to store any information up to 137 bytes and pass this information to NFC compatible devices through touch. At the moment, it is empty but you can put information on there using an app such as 'NFC Tools'. Some examples of how to use it include:

Contact details
Put contact details like your phone number of e-mail address on there to use as 'business card'
Payment method
Some study associations allow payment through NFC. You can use this keychain as payment method.
Home automation
Integrating NFC into your home automation setup can allow for extra functionality in your smart home.
Link sharing
You can also just put a link on there of your own website or let's say your favourite synth-pop song from 1987.
